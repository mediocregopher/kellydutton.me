About Kelly

I have always been passionate about learning and education. After graduating from the University of Florida with a triple major, I conducted management and marketing activities for a nationwide residential property management company. I then returned to graduate school and completed my master's degree in Political Science with a specialization in Security and Diplomacy. Since then, I have worked as a freelance English editor and copywriter for both individuals and businesses worldwide. Additionally, to further my understanding of how the English language is viewed from the perspective of non-native-speakers, I also successfully completed a Teaching English as a Foreign Language (TEFL) certificate in 2018.
